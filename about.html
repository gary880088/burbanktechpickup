Who we are · Burbank & Greater LA

About Burbank Electronics Pickup

Local. Simple. Reliable. We're a local electronics recycling pickup company serving homes and businesses across Burbank and Greater Los Angeles. We come to you — no corporate layer, no automated systems, no drop-off. A real local operation.

What we do

Electronics recycling pickup for homes & businesses

We pick up electronics — laptops, computers, monitors, phones, tablets, game consoles, servers, and more. Working or broken. Mixed loads welcome. We do the loading, we handle the hauling, and we deliver the equipment to reputable recycling partners.

We started this service because people across Burbank and Los Angeles have a real problem: old equipment piles up, and there aren't many reliable local options that make recycling it simple.

So we built a straightforward pickup service. Call us, tell us what you have, and we come get it. That's the whole thing.
