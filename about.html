Who we are

Local. Simple. Reliable.

We're a local electronics pickup company serving homes and businesses across Greater Los Angeles. No corporate layer. No automated systems. A real local operation.

What we do

Electronics pickup for everyone

We pick up electronics — laptops, desktops, monitors, phones, servers, and more. Working or broken. Mixed loads welcome. We do the loading, we handle the hauling, and we take care of the equipment responsibly.

We started this service because people in Los Angeles have a real problem: old equipment piles up and there aren't many reliable local options that make the process simple.

So we built a straightforward pickup service. Call us, tell us what you have, we come get it. That's the whole thing.
